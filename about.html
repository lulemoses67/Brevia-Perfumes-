About Us

We are create and distribute Perfumes, Colognes, essentail oils, or other scents-related products. we focus on creating unique fragrances and retailing fragrances at our shop in Masaka town and online. Brevia caters for various markets, such as Luxury fragrances, Mass market fragrances and custom fragrances. We understand the fragrances chemistry, marketing and consumer preferences to create and sell appealing scents that capture the essence of a personal style.
